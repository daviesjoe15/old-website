University Project

Loughborough University holds a unique module (design week) where successful brands set a design brief with a deadline of one week.

The Problem

Their problem was that people order convenience food because they don't have the time to prepare meals for themselves.

The companies judge our ability to apply a balanced and appropriate design process for the problem and our design communication skills.

My design process

I designed an MVP (minimal viable product) which evolved through extensive market research and user testing.

I drafted detailed wireframes to achieve a polished product ready for release.

I developed the company branding and with it a cohesive style guide.

Using proven research methods including A/B testing and user observation we produced a more efficient user flow and beautiful interface.

Meticulous design communication between myself and app developers in Ukraine was critical for the successful launch of the app.
