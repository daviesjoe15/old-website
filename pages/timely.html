University Project

Loughborough University holds a unique module (design week) where successful brands set a design brief with a deadline of one week.

The Problem

Their problem was that people order convenience food because they don't have the time to prepare meals for themselves.

The companies judge our ability to apply a balanced and appropriate design process for the problem and our design communication skills.

My design process

Market Research

Brain storming ideas

User observation

Role play cooking

I took logical steps towards identifying insights and comparing them to products on the market, this lead towards concept generation and then a realistic product solution.

My research clarified that an inability to effectively track cooking and preparation times for different foods resulted in wasted time.

This was solved by introducing a set of timers that could easily be assigned to individual cooking tasks.
